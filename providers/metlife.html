MetLife Dental Insurance Plans for Kids

Learn about MetLife dental plans, often featuring no waiting periods for major procedures.

MetLife is a good option for families seeking immediate access to a wider range of dental services, as some plans may not have waiting periods for major procedures. They typically offer 100% coverage for in-network preventive care like cleanings and exams.

Highlights: No waiting periods for most major procedures (on select plans), 100% for in-network cleanings/exams, Orthodontics coverage up to age 19 (High plan).
